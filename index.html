Yasufumi Moriya

I'm a PhD student at School of Computing, Dublin City University, ADAPT Centre.

I'm under supervision of Prof. Gareth Jones.

My research focuses on integration of multimodal signals into automatic speech recognition (ASR) and spoken content retrieval (SCR). Every minute 300 hours of videos(!) are uploaded to YouTube. We need a good retrieval system to find a video, that we are interested in, from a gigantic pile of videos.

SCR systems often rely on ASR transcription; hence improvement of ASR a key to better SCR.
